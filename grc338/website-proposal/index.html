Introduction

Butter & Bloom Bakery

Butter & Bloom Bakery is a small, family-owned bakery located in San Luis Obispo, California. We specialize in fresh artisan breads, flaky pastries, custom cakes, cookies, and handcrafted coffee drinks. Every item is baked fresh each morning using high-quality ingredients and time-honored recipes. Whether customers stop by for breakfast, an afternoon treat, or a special celebration, Butter & Bloom Bakery offers a cozy atmosphere and homemade baked goods that bring people together.

Target audience

The primary audience for Sweet Crumbs Bakery includes local residents, college students, families, and visitors looking for fresh baked goods and coffee.

Most visitors come to the website to view the bakery's menu, check business hours, find the bakery's location, learn about its story, and decide what to order before visiting. Customers want a simple website that quickly shows what products are available and where the bakery is located.

About

Our Story: Sweet Crumbs Bakery began with one family's dream of bringing fresh, handcrafted baking to the local community. What started as weekend farmers market sales quickly grew into a neighborhood bakery where customers could enjoy fresh bread, pastries, and cakes every day. We believe baking is about more than recipes—it's about creating moments people remember. Every loaf of bread, cookie, and pastry is made fresh daily using simple, high-quality ingredients. Our team is passionate about creating delicious baked goods while providing friendly service in a welcoming space for everyone.
